Background

Suppose you were able to turn a 1920’s motor vehicle into a time machine. It is of paramount importance to be able to properly estimate stopping distance at various speeds.

Why is proper distance estimation important?

In the Back To The Future Movie, the stopping distance for the vehicle is not properly calculated and Mart McFly crashes into…

  • a barn after traveling back in time
  • a movie theater after traveling forward in time.

A stopping distance calculator would enable time travelers to properly plan travel. At 88 mph, the flux capacitor activates time travel, which is indicated by a blue line on the following plot.
